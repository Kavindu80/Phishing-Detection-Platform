How to get your JWT Token

  1. Open your PhishGuard web application and log in to your account
  2. Open browser DevTools (F12) → Go to Application tab
  3. In the sidebar, expand Local Storage → click on your domain
  4. Look for a key like auth_token or access_token
  5. Copy the token value and paste it in the field above
  6. Alternative: Check the Network tab during login for the /api/auth/login response
